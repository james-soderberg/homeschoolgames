Keep the Web Alive

🌿Grass→ 🐇Rabbits→ 🦊You→ 🦅Hawk

You're the fox. Hunt rabbits to survive — but the rabbits keep the grass in check, and the grass feeds the rabbits. Eat too many and the herd vanishes and you starve. Eat too few and the rabbits strip the meadow bare and crash. Hold the whole web in balance — the longer it thrives, the higher your score. And flee the hawk 🦅.

Steer with the mouse / finger or WASD / arrows. Move over a rabbit to eat it.
