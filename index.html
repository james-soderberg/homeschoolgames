HomeschoolGames is a free collection of browser-based educational games that turn everyday skill practice into play. There's nothing to buy or install — just pick a game and start learning. Great for homeschool lessons, classroom warm-ups, road trips, and screen time you can feel good about.

Frequently asked questions

Are the games really free?

Yes. Every game on HomeschoolGames is 100% free to play in your web browser. There is no signup, no account, no download, and no ads.

What subjects do the games cover?

Typing, math, spelling and vocabulary, history, geography, science, and Bible. New learning games are added regularly.

Do the games work on Chromebooks, tablets, and phones?

Yes. The games run in any modern web browser on Chromebooks, laptops, iPads, tablets, and phones — there is nothing to install.

Are these games good for homeschoolers?

They are built for homeschooling families: quick, focused skill practice — typing speed, math facts, spelling, geography, and more — that kids actually want to play.

What ages are the games for?

Most games suit roughly ages 6 to 14, from early elementary through middle school, with difficulty that scales up as kids improve.
